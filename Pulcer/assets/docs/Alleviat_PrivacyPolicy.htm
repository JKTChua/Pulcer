Alleviathas implemented various security measures to protect against the unintended loss, disclosure, misuse or alteration of the information under our control while using our service.

1. We do not store cookies on your machine. Cookies are small pieces of computer code that identify you and your computer. Many sites use cookies to identify you when you come to a website, especially if the site or application is personalized for you. Because certain types of cookies can allow other people access to your information, we have decided not to store them after you log out of your account.

2. You must log in to Alleviat’s, Inc.,application any time you want to access your personal information. In order to view your account on a website or on any mobile or tablet-based application, you will be asked to log in with your email address and unique password. This password is personal to you and should not be shared with anyone. If you leave the website or application, you should be sure to log off. In the event that you forget to log off and leave your computer or if there is no activity on the Plus account for more than 5 minutes, you will automatically be logged off and will be asked again to log in with your password as an added measure of security.

3. All of your personal information is encrypted when it is stored in and transmitted by our system. "Encrypted" means that your personal information is "scrambled" with special complex codes so that no other individual or computer can read it without the password to "unlock" the code.

4. You have full control over your information. You can change any piece of information in your password-protected account at any time within the Profile section. Changes are processed in real time.

5.         Alleviat, Inc.,monitors the limited number of our employees that can potentially access your information. 6. We will never ask for your password in an unsolicited phone call or email- do not ever give it out.

7.         All the information Alleviat, Inc.,collects is hosted on secure servers stored behind firewalls that are backed up regularly to ensure minimal loss of information in the event of a system failure or disaster.

THE HEALTH INSURANCE PORTABILITY AND ACCOUNTABILITY ACT ("HIPAA") AND THE HEALTH INFORMATION FOR TECHNOLOGY FOR ECONOMIC AND CLINICAL HEALTH ("HITECH") ACT

Although many people assume that HIPAA protects all personal health information, however obtained and wherever stored, HIPAA is actually somewhat limited in its applicability. HIPAA governs only "covered entities" and their "business associates." Covered entities under HIPAA include (i) health plans (insurance companies, HMOs, etc.); (ii) health care clearinghouses (entities that convert health information into or out of standard formats for billing or other purposes); and (iii) most health care providers (physicians, hospitals, etc.). Business associates are entities that contract with covered entities to provide certain services that involve the use or disclosure of individually identifiable health information.

ALLEVIATE, INC.is not a covered entity under HIPAA but does, in some circumstances, act as a business associate. In such circumstances, Plus will comply with the rules promulgated by the Department of Health and Human Services under both HIPAA and HITECH for business associates, including the rules relating to security of personal health information and requiring notification of security breaches of unsecured protected health information. See our Security Policy for more detail.
